Tierra Mia Coffee isn’t just another coffee shop — it’s a love letter to Latin American coffee traditions, from the highlands of Chiapas to the bustling cafeterías of Miami and Los Angeles. The Tierra Mia menu embodies a unique fusion: high-quality, small-batch espresso paired with ingredients like horchata, dulce de leche, guava, and plant-based alternatives that honor heritage while embracing modern tastes. 💡 Signature Drinks Deep Dive

The legendary Horchata Latte remains a fan favorite — made from scratch with rice milk, cinnamon, and vanilla bean, then blended with a double shot of Tierra Mia's signature espresso. It’s subtly sweet, creamy, and sprinkled with cinnamon. The Cubano is another iconic beverage: espresso whipped with demerara sugar to create a thick, caramel-like foam layer. Pair it with a warm Pastelito de Guayaba for an authentic Miami-style breakfast. For health-conscious guests, the Matcha Horchata provides antioxidants with less caffeine. And cold brew lovers can try nitro cold brew infused with vanilla and cinnamon notes.

⚡ Nutritional highlights & customizations: Most milk-based drinks can be made with oat, soy, almond, or non-fat milk. Sugar-free vanilla and caramel syrups available. Vegan options: Horchata latte (request plant milk), spinach empanada, black bean soup, and the avocado tostada (skip crema). Gluten-free: cold brew, espresso drinks, corn tostada, and smoothies.

Beyond the core offerings, Tierra Mia releases limited-time flavors that align with Latin American holidays and seasons.
